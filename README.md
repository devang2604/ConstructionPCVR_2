# 🏗️ VR-Based Construction Safety Training Simulator

## 🎯 Objective

To improve safety awareness in construction environments by providing an immersive, interactive training system where users can identify, analyze, and respond to real-world hazards.  

This solution aims to move beyond passive learning (videos, posters, lectures) and instead build **intuitive hazard recognition skills** through hands-on virtual experience.

## 🌍 Why This Matters

Construction is one of the most accident-prone industries worldwide. Traditional training methods often fail to simulate:

- Real-world stress and urgency  
- Spatial complexity of job sites  
- Immediate consequences of unsafe actions  

As a result, workers may understand safety rules in theory but struggle to apply them in practice.

This project addresses that gap by creating a **realistic VR-based training environment** where users actively engage with hazards instead of just learning about them.

---

## 💡 Our Approach

We are building a virtual construction site where users:

- Explore a 3D environment  
- Observe ongoing activities  
- Identify unsafe conditions  
- Take corrective actions  

Each scenario is designed to replicate common on-site dangers and enforce proper safety protocols through interaction.

---

## 🚀 Implemented Features

### ⛑ Missing Safety Gear (PPE Detection)

- Multiple workers present in the scene  
- Some workers are missing essential safety equipment:
  - Helmet  
  - Safety jacket  

**Goal:**  
Users must identify what is wrong in the scene.

**Focus:**  
Visual awareness and basic safety compliance.

---

### ⚡ Exposed Electrical Hazard

- Hanging electrical wires  
- Visible sparking  
- Water present nearby (high-risk condition)  

**Goal:**  
Users identify and neutralize the hazard.

**Focus:**  
Recognizing dangerous electrical setups and acting quickly.

---

### 🧪 Chemical Leak Scenario

- A leaking chemical drum  
- Workers standing nearby in unsafe proximity  

**Flow:**
1. Identify the hazard  
2. Move workers away from danger  
3. Call a hazmat professional  
4. Hazard gets resolved  

**Focus:**  
Multi-step decision making and following correct safety procedures.

---

## 🧠 Planned Enhancements

### 🚧 Open Edge Hazard

- Unsafe construction edges without:
  - Guard rails  
  - Safety nets  

**Goal:**  
Highlight fall risks and enforce preventive measures.

---

### 🔮 Future Scope

- Falling object hazards  
- Fire and emergency scenarios  
- Heavy machinery risk simulation  
- Performance tracking and scoring  
- Adaptive training based on user behavior  
- Multi-user training environments (trainer + trainee)

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Engine:** Unity (XR Interaction Toolkit)  
- **VR Support:** OpenXR  
- **3D Assets:** Sketchfab, Quixel Megascans, Custom Blender models  
- **Scripting:** C#  
- **Target Platform:** Meta Quest (Standalone VR)

---

## 🧭 Vision

The long-term goal is to develop a scalable training system that can be:

- Deployed across construction sites and training institutes  
- Used as a standardized safety training module  
- Adapted for different industries beyond construction  

By combining immersion with interaction, this solution aims to make safety training **practical, memorable, and effective**.
